Frequently Asked Questions

How can I order?

You can order easily using our online platform. When you find a product you need, you can add it to cart, login and go through the ordering process. After the order is ready, you will receive order summary to your email. Order summary will also be stored to your account.
You can also easily make reorders afterwards by clicking the “reorder” button on any of your previously made orders. After clicking the “reorder” button the cart will open, and you can change quantities or products.

What information should I input when ordering?

Our online ordering system will ask for all the important information you should submit. If you have a phone number, please remember to submit it. This will make sure the shipment is not delayed.

How can I change delivery address?

You can easily change your delivery address during checkout.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges are dependent on the shipment requirements and differ from shop to shop. If the products on your order are due to special requirements (for example dry ice) extra fee will be added to the shipment charges. You can see the shipping fees on the checkout process before the payment is made.

Do you have the product in stock?

All the products which are shown on our site are available. Order lead time depends on the products and quantities.

Can I return a product?

If you want to return a product, please contact the shop you have ordered from.

How to Sell on Espace Nepal?

Register as a seller, go to the sign-up page and click on 'Become-seller'

Do I need a company to sell on Espace Nepal?

No, you don't need to have a registered business to start selling on Espace. You can set up an Espace seller central account with your personal info or business info.

How can I retrieve my password?

You can retrieve your password by clicking “forgot password?”. Instruction on password retrieval will be send to your email.

Security

Our web store is secured with SSL certificate. This means the information you input is encrypted and it will not be available for third parties.
